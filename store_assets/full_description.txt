🎯 WHY PDCAAS MATTERS

PDCAAS (Protein Digestibility Corrected Amino Acid Score) is the most widely used standard by the WHO and FDA to measure protein quality. A score of 1.0 means complete protein. Many products score far lower.

• Whey protein: 1.0 (excellent)
• Egg: 1.0 (excellent)  
• Beef: 0.92 (great)
• Soy: 0.91 (great)
• Pea protein: 0.73 (good)
• Collagen: 0.0 (zero usable protein!)
